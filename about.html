About Me

Where Inner Confidence Meets Authenticity & Visual Storytelling

Carissa Michae is a professional model with experience across commercial, editorial, and lifestyle work. Known for a calm on-set presence and strong direction-following, she brings a polished, approachable energy to every shoot—helping brands communicate clearly through confident movement and authentic expression. She is comfortable working both independently and within collaborative teams, adapting quickly to creative direction while maintaining a consistent, professional presence. Known for her reliability and professionalism on set, Carissa is punctual, communicative, and easy to work with. Whether working on set or on location, Carissa aims to create imagery that feels confident, intentional, and visually compelling—supporting each project with clarity, presence, and professionalism.
